Facilitated Transformation Game

Playing the Transformation game is and effective way to:
  • clarify important issues
  • resolve conflicts and release negative patterns
  • enhance relationships
  • release fears and open to new perspectives
  • find new directions and move forward with ease and grace
  • achieve your goals
  • shine in your radiance
    •

    How does it work?
    You will start by setting a clear focus for playing the game, an intention that is important for you at this time. Then on a board game you will move along the symbolic representation of your life path, using a dye to determine how many steps.
    The game will mirror your attitudes and unconscious believes about the issue you bring to it, therefore offering you the possibility for transformation and change. While playing you will move through experiences in which you will able to gain new isights and awareness that will lead to progress or come across obstacles that may uncover some old pain, so that you can learn how to release it.
    You will be empowered to discover and tap into your strengths as well as the metaphysical help which is always available when we chose to recognise it.

    Cost: £50 per hour. Minimum three hours needed.
